# The Broken Exit

Welcome to The Broken Exit, a psychological horror loop experience developed using Unreal Engine 5 for PC.

Trapped inside a repeating nightmare, the player relives the same space over and over — but something is always different. What starts as a normal bedroom slowly descends into a disturbing and surreal experience.

There is no escape through skill or puzzle-solving… only through experiencing all seven loops.

# Gameplay

In The Broken Exit, the player wakes up in a bedroom and walks toward the main door.

Each time the door is opened:

- The screen fades out
- The player resets back in the bedroom
- The environment changes

This loop repeats 7 times.

With each loop:

- The environment becomes darker and more distorted
- Reality breaks further
- The atmosphere grows increasingly unsettling

After the 7th loop:

- The main door finally opens
- The camera moves outside
- A bright white light fills the scene
- Calm ambient audio replaces the tension
- The experience ends with the title reveal


# Features
- Narrative Loop Experience
  - 7 consecutive loops forming a complete psychological journey
  - Designed to feel like a recurring nightmare
- No Challenge-Based Gameplay
  - No puzzles, no difficulty barriers
  - Purely experience-driven progression
- Evolving Environment
  - Flickering lights
  - Object displacement
  - Floating and falling objects
  - Increasing visual distortion
- Psychological Horror Elements
  - Subtle and escalating environmental storytelling
  - Horror text appearing on walls
  - Fog, color shifts, and lighting changes
  - Sound design that builds discomfort over time
- Atmospheric Ending
  - Strong contrast between horror and calm
  - Bright white light as a form of release
  - Minimalistic but impactful conclusion


# How to Experience
- Wake Up
  You begin in the bedroom.
- Observe
  Notice what has changed — even small details matter.
- Move Forward
  Walk toward the main door.
- Take the Key
- Repeat
  The loop continues, but the world becomes more unstable.
- End the Cycle
  After the 7th loop, step into the light.
